How to play Koi-Koi (quick rules)
Each round you're dealt 8 cards, 8 cards sit face-up on the field, and the rest form a draw deck.

Your turn: play a card from your hand. If it shares a month with a field card, you capture both. If it matches nothing, it stays on the field. Then draw the top deck card and repeat the same matching against the field.

Yaku: certain combinations of captured cards score points (e.g. three "bright" cards, five ribbons, boar+deer+butterfly, etc). The moment you complete or improve a yaku, you choose: Koi-Koi (keep playing for more points, but doubles the round's score if anyone scores again) or Shobu (bank the points now and end the round).

Highest total score after all rounds wins.
